What we do

As a specialist in ship management solutions, we pride ourselves on being efficient, safe and trustworthy. We maintain the highest standards of health and safety, as well as striving to minimise any adverse impact on the environment.

We are a leading, independent manager of oil tankers, bulk carriers and LPG vessels. We provide high-quality, modern vessels, run by best-in-class seafarers, and supported by a competent and motivated team ashore.
